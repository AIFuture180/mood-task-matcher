Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 05, 2025

Welcome to Mood-to-Task Matcher! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your experience on our website is safe and enjoyable. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, https://www.moodtask.org.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., through a future email subscription feature). However, we do use localStorage in your browser to store non-personal data, such as:

  • Your mood selections and history (e.g., "stressed on April 05, 2025").
  • Your favorited tasks (e.g., tasks you choose to save for later).

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. It helps us provide a personalized experience, such as showing your mood history or favorite tasks.

2. Third-Party Services

Our website includes links to third-party services, such as:

  • Affiliate links (e.g., Online Therapy banner ads).
  • Social media sharing options (e.g., Twitter share button).
  • Buy Me a Coffee widget for donations.

These third-party services may collect their own data according to their privacy policies. We are not responsible for the data practices of these third parties, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies before interacting with them.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We do not currently use cookies or tracking technologies. However, third-party services (e.g., Google AdSense, if approved) may use cookies to serve ads or track user behavior. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

The data stored in localStorage is used solely to enhance your experience on our website, such as:

  • Displaying your mood history and favorite tasks.
  • Providing personalized task recommendations based on your mood selections.

We do not share, sell, or disclose this data to any third parties.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device via localStorage, it is as secure as your device. We recommend keeping your device secure to protect your data. If you clear your browserâ€™s storage or use a different device, your mood history and favorites will not be accessible.

6. Your Choices

You can clear your mood history and favorites at any time using the "Clear History" button on our homepage. This will remove all data stored in localStorage.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@moodtask.org.
