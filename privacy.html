Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Mood-to-Task Matcher! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your information is handled. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website, moodtask.org. Our site provides mood-based tasks and informational content to help improve your day, and we do not directly collect personal information from users. However, third-party services we use may collect certain data, as described below.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect or store personal information (such as your name, email address, or payment details) on our servers. However, the following types of information may be collected through third-party services:

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about how users interact with our website, such as page views, time spent on pages, and button clicks (e.g., on the "Tasks to Impact" page). This data does not identify you personally but helps us understand user behavior to improve our services.
  • Device and Browser Information: Google Analytics may collect information about your device, browser type, IP address (in an anonymized form), and operating system to provide insights into how our site is accessed.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: See the "Cookies and Tracking Technologies" section below for details.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected through third-party services is used to:

  • Analyze website usage to improve our content and user experience (e.g., understanding which tasks or blog posts are most popular).
  • Monitor the performance of our website and ensure it functions properly.
  • Provide insights into how users interact with features like the mood matcher or "Tasks to Impact" buttons.

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties except as described in this policy (e.g., with Google Analytics or affiliate partners).

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use Google Analytics to collect usage data, which may involve the use of cookies and similar tracking technologies. Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help analyze how users interact with our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to track page views, session duration, and events (e.g., button clicks). This data is anonymized and does not identify you personally.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services on our website, which may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track website usage and user interactions (e.g., button clicks on the "Tasks to Impact" page). Google Analytics may collect data such as your IP address (anonymized), browser type, and device information. For more details, see Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Online Therapy (Affiliate Program): Our site includes affiliate links to Online Therapy. If you click these links, Online Therapy may collect information about your visit or purchase through their tracking system. We do not have access to this data. Please review Online Therapy’s Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Buy Me a Coffee: We provide a link to Buy Me a Coffee for donations. If you choose to donate, Buy Me a Coffee may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and payment details. We do not have access to this information. Please review Buy Me a Coffee’s Privacy Policy for more details.
  • Google Ads: Our site uses Google Ads for monetization. Google Ads may use cookies to serve relevant advertisements based on your browsing history. For more information, see Google’s Privacy Policy.

Affiliate Disclosure

Mood-to-Task Matcher participates in affiliate programs, including with Online Therapy. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. These links lead to third-party websites, which have their own privacy policies and data collection practices. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites, and we encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal information.

Data Security

We do not directly collect or store personal data on our servers, so there is no risk of data breaches involving personal information on our end. However, third-party services like Google Analytics, Online Therapy, and Buy Me a Coffee have their own security measures in place to protect your data. We encourage you to review their privacy policies for more information on how they secure your information.

User Rights

Since we do not directly collect personal data, we do not store or process personal information that would require us to fulfill data subject requests (e.g., data deletion or access requests). However, you have the following rights regarding data collected by third-party services:

  • Opt-Out of Google Analytics: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.
  • Manage Cookies: You can disable cookies in your browser settings to prevent tracking by Google Analytics or Google Ads.
  • Third-Party Data: If you have concerns about data collected by third-party services (e.g., Online Therapy, Buy Me a Coffee), you can contact those services directly to exercise your rights under applicable privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

Children’s Privacy

Mood-to-Task Matcher is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to a third-party service through our site, please contact us at support@moodtask.org, and we will work with the third-party service to address the issue.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this policy will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle your information.
