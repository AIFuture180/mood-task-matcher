Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Mood-to-Task Matcher, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

What We Collect

We collect minimal data to improve your experience. This includes:

  • Mood History: When you use our tool, we store your mood selections and task history in your browser’s local storage to provide personalized recommendations. This data is not sent to our servers.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about how users interact with our site, such as page views and session duration. This helps us improve our services.

How We Use Your Data

Your mood history is used solely to provide personalized task recommendations and is stored locally on your device. We do not share or sell your data to third parties. Analytics data is used to understand user behavior and improve our website.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and Buy Me a Coffee. These services may collect data as outlined in their respective privacy policies. We also include affiliate links (e.g., Online Therapy, Blueprint by Bryan Johnson), which may track referrals but do not collect personal information from you.

Your Choices

You can clear your mood history at any time using the “Clear History” button on our home page. You can also disable cookies in your browser settings to limit analytics tracking, though this may affect your experience on our site.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@moodtask.org.
