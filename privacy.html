Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Mood-to-Task Matcher, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We use local storage to save your mood history and favorite tasks. This data is stored on your device and is not sent to our servers. We do not collect any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like Google AdSense, Buy Me a Coffee, and affiliate links (e.g., Blueprint, Online Therapy). These services may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Buy Me a Coffee Privacy Policy
  • Online Therapy Privacy Policy

Cookies

We use cookies through Google AdSense to serve personalized ads. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of data breaches on our end. However, we recommend keeping your device secure to protect your local storage data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

If you have any questions, please contact us via our blog or on Twitter.
