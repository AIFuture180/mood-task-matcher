5 Quick Mindfulness Exercises for Stress

Stress can feel overwhelming, but mindfulness exercises can help you find calm in just a few minutes. Here are five quick practices to try:

1. Deep Breathing

Take 5 deep breaths, inhaling for 4 seconds and exhaling for 4 seconds. Imagine your stress melting away with each exhale.

2. Grounding with Your Senses

Name 5 things you can see, 4 things you can touch, 3 things you can hear, 2 things you can smell, and 1 thing you can taste. This helps bring you back to the present moment.

3. Body Scan

Close your eyes and focus on each part of your body, starting from your toes and moving up to your head. Notice any tension and breathe into it to release.
