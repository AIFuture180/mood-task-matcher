How to Create a Daily Mood Check-In Routine

A daily mood check-in can help you stay in tune with your emotions and improve your mental health. Here’s how to create a routine:

1. Set a Time

Choose a consistent time each day, like morning or evening, to check in with your mood.

2. Use a Tool

Use our Mood-to-Task Matcher tool to select your mood and get a quick activity to match how you’re feeling.

3. Reflect on Your Mood

Take a moment to think about why you’re feeling this way. Are there patterns or triggers you can identify?

4. Take Action

Do the suggested activity from Mood-to-Task Matcher, or try a mindfulness exercise to shift your mood if needed.

5. Track Your Progress

Keep a journal or use our mood history feature to track your moods over time and see how your routine impacts your well-being.

Start your daily mood check-in today and see the difference it makes in your mental health!
