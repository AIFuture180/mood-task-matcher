About Mood-to-Task Matcher

Our Mission

At Mood-to-Task Matcher, we believe that small actions can make a big difference in how you feel. Our mission is to help you manage your emotions and boost your mood with quick, fun activities tailored to how you’re feeling.

How It Works

Simply select your current mood on our home page, and we’ll suggest a playful task to help you feel better. Whether you’re tired, stressed, or happy, we’ve got a task for you!

Why We Created This Tool

We wanted to create a simple, accessible way for anyone to improve their mental health, even on the busiest days. Our tasks are designed to be quick and easy, so you can fit a little self-care into your routine.

Have feedback or ideas? We’d love to hear from you! Reach out via our blog or on Twitter.
