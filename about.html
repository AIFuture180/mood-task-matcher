About Mood-to-Task Matcher

At Mood-to-Task Matcher, our mission is to help you improve your day with quick, fun tasks tailored to your mood. Whether you're feeling tired, stressed, or happy, we believe that small actions can make a big difference in your well-being.

Our Story

We started this project to make mental health and productivity accessible to everyone. By matching simple tasks to your current mood, we aim to provide a moment of joy, relaxation, or motivation whenever you need it.
